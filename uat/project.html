Our Projects

“Muslim Information Network of Australasia (MINA)” completed 10 years of successfully serving the society inhibiting Australian and Islamic values benefitting the people of this wonderful land.

Multi-faith Eid program

Successfully organised the Eid program with other faith since 2016, attendees ranging from social leaders such as Member of Parliament, Mayors, councillors, Victoria police officers to extended family of the MINA members like colleagues and friends from other faith. This program helped us in blending with larger society and breaking communal shackles.

Eid ul Fitr & Eid ul Adha

Annual Islamic celebration marking the end of Ramadan, the Islamic holy month of fasting. We conduct this day long program at a pre booked community centre with around 50 families participating the festival.

Melbourne Immigration Transit Accommodation (MITA)

Periodically conduct programs at MITA supporting and contributing to the betterment of people at this detention centre. The response was overwhelming that induce urge to contribute further to society especially on humanitarian aspects.

  • To invite towards the noble way
  • To promote spiritual and character building
  • To offer Social Service
  • To become and create role models

NLP Course

A 2 day intense NLP course to help our community to use my mind ablities to fullest and become better human beings.

Forest Camping

Camping with families to spend some relax and enjoying time with nature and build strong friend other members and respect for the Australian Natural Life

Word to Word Quran Classes

To better understand the Quran, a word to word course is provide to the community to understand the Quran word by word.
