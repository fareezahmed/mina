About Us

“Muslim Information Network of Australasia (MINA)” completed 10 years of successfully serving the society inhibiting Australian and Islamic values benefitting the people of this wonderful land.

Our Vision

To facilitate and empower Muslim community to utilise their full potential by contributing profusely to the Australian society.

Our Mission

To drive community well-being by exhibiting leadership and team work which lead to purposeful life.

MINA is a not for profit organisation managed by committee of management overseeing the activities and ensure it leads to the vision and objectives outlined. This association comprises group of like-minded families of Islamic faith from diverse demographic background across Indian subcontinent.

MINA conducts weekly gatherings conducting various lectures acquiring knowledge and encouraging implementing in day to day life yielding social benefits. Executive committee gathers every month to review and refine the ongoing programs and to plan the future activities' in a better way.

Some Projects and Events

We engage in many projects and events to support the local communities such as Multi-faith Eid program, Eid ul Fitr, Eid ul Adha, Melbourne Immigration Transit Accommodation (MITA), NLP, Word to Word etc
